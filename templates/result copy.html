Steak Surprise

Step 1

Mince garlic and make marinade by mixing 4 tablespoons oil, 2 tablespoons vinegar, and half of the garlic in a small bowl.

Step 2

Pour marinade in glass baking dish, add steaks, and turn to coat both sides. Marinate at room temperature for 30 minutes. I can set a reminder if you say Alexa, start timer.

Step 3

Slice vegetables, heat 2 tablespoons of oil in skillet at medium heat, and add vegetables and remaining garlic to skillet. Saute for 8 minutes.

Step 4

Add 1 cup of broth plus salt and pepper to taste. Increase heat to boiling. Boil for 5 minutes.

Step 5

Turn off the burner and cover skillet for now. Let's clean while we wait for the marinade timer.

Step 6

Preheat your broiler while you remove the meat from the marinade and sprinkle it with salt and pepper.

Step 7

Broil steak and flip halfway through. 5 minutes per side for rare, 8 minutes for well-done.

Step 8

Put steaks on a cutting board and let rest for 5 minutes while bringing vegetables to a simmer.

Step 9

Slice steaks against the grain. Artfully arrange steak slices and vegetables on platter. Pour some of the skillet juices on top and then sprinkle with cheese and parsley. Enjoy your meal. I wish I could taste it.

Step 10

Enjoy!
